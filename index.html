How To Play?

Launch the app and you will find home screen where in centre you will find Start Playing. Below Start Playing you can see your highest Score till date. If you tap on Start Playing you game will start.

In the game there are eight different levels. In each level you will have to catch as many balls as you can. You can check your score in bottom. After completion of every level you will get an alert showing your level completion and will ask if you want to play next level. Each level speeds up the challenge.
