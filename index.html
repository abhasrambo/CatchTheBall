How To Play?

Once the game launches, you will see the screen with Start Playing. This screen is your home screen. Below you can see your highest score.

Once you select Start Playing you will go to your game screen. You have to catch as many balls as you can in each level. There are total eight levels. In each level the balls gets faster and the challenge increases.

In the game screen you can see your current session score. After completion of each level you will get an alert displaying which level you have completed. Once you have completed all levels of the game you will get option to replay and beat your previous best score.
